BlackSnap

The Client

BlackSnap is a photography studio based in Atlanta, Georgia. Founded with the passion and eye for beauty of Dee, this photography company excels in emphasizing the beauty and power behind a scene. They use this to work with a variety of brands, models, and non-profit groups such as Kleepstyle, All I Want is Everything, and the FreeHER campaign

Overview

I provided BlackSnap a marketing and development package that included the creation of a brand-kit and the execution brand material such as business cards. Later expanding to designing, developing, and maintaining a website to foster connection between the client’s work with potential consumers. We also provided insight in expansion opportunities and asset organization.

The Brandkit

I was assigned the task of developing a brand kit that aligns with the business’s core identity and direction. By incorporating the concepts of Engagement, Radiance, Community, and Perspective, I curated a selection of fonts, colors, and typographic treatments. These elements not only highlight the exceptional work previously done but also serve to represent the company’s future objectives.

The Website

I created a seamless interactive experience that works in tandem with the BlackSnap brand and identity. Emphasizing image quality and display, this website frames the quality product and affords the ability to contact the business for inqueries.

The Assets

To support BlackSnap's business endeavors, I created graphic design assets for the promotion of the brand, including business cards, social media posts, and posters.
